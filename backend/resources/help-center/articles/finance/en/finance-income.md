# Finance Income

Finance Income records incoming funds and posts them to accounts.

## How this page is used
- Select an income category and finance account.
- Add payer or donor details if used.
- Save the transaction for reporting.

## Typical flow
1. Create income categories and accounts with starting balances.
2. Record income transactions.
3. Review income reports and dashboards.
