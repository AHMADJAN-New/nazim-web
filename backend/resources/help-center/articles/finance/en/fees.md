# Fees

Fees manages student billing from structures to payments.

## How this page is used
- Define fee structures and assign them to students or classes.
- Record payments and apply exceptions when needed.
- Use reports to track collections and balances.

## Typical flow
1. Create fee structures.
2. Assign fees and collect payments.
3. Review fee reports and dashboards.
