# Staff Registration Report

The Staff Registration Report lets you view and export detailed staff data in PDF or Excel format. Use this report when you need to share staff registration information with authorities, print records for filing, or analyze staff across your organization.

---

## How to Access

1. From **Staff Management**, click the **"Registration Report"** button in the page header.
2. Or from the sidebar, go to **Reports** → **Staff Reports** → **Staff Registrations Report** (route: `/reports/staff-registrations`).

---

## What You Can Do

- **Filter** — Narrow staff by School, Status, Staff Type, or search by name/ID/phone/email.
- **View details** — Click the eye icon on any row to see full staff profile (personal info, contact, location, education, employment).
- **Export PDF** — Generate a branded PDF report with all columns (Staff Code, Employee ID, Name, Status, Contact, Education, Employment, Location, etc.).
- **Export Excel** — Generate an Excel spreadsheet with the same data for further analysis.

---

## Requirements

- **Permission** — You need `staff_reports.read` to access the page.
- **School** — A school must be selected (or use your default school) for PDF/Excel export to work.
