# Library Reports

The Library Reports page provides analytics and exportable data for your library: summary cards, Books Report (with search and category filter), Overdue Books, Due Soon, Loan History (with date range), and Most Borrowed. School administrators and librarians use this page to monitor totals, find overdue and due-soon loans, review loan history, and export the books list to CSV.

---

## Page Overview

When you open the Library Reports page, you will see:

### Summary Cards

- **Total Books** — Number of book titles. Description: total copies.
- **Available Copies** — Copies currently available. Description: how many on loan.
- **Active Loans** — Open loans. Description: "Currently loaned out".
- **Overdue** — Loans past due date. Description: "Books past due date".
- **Due Soon** — Loans due in the next 7 days. Description: "Due in next 7 days".
- **Loan History** — Count of loans in the selected date range. Description: "In selected date range".

### Tabs

1. **Books Report** — Filterable, paginated list of books with optional CSV export.
2. **Overdue Books** — List of overdue loans with pagination.
3. **Due Soon** — List of loans due in the next 7 days with pagination.
4. **Loan History** — All loans in a date range (From Date, To Date) with pagination.
5. **Most Borrowed** — Books ranked by number of loans, with pagination.

---

## Tab 1: Books Report

- **Filters** — Search (title, author, ISBN, book number), Category (All or specific). Reset clears filters.
- **Table (or list)** — Books with details such as title, author, category, price, copies, etc. Pagination (e.g. 10, 25, 50, 100 per page).
- **Export** — **Export CSV** (or similar) downloads a CSV of the **filtered** books (e.g. title, author, ISBN, book number, category, price, total copies, available copies, default loan days). Filename may include date.

---

## Tab 2: Overdue Books

- **Content** — Cards or rows for each overdue loan: book title, due date, copy code, author. Badge or label "Overdue".
- **Pagination** — Per-page selector and page navigation. Empty state: "No overdue books. Great job!".

---

## Tab 3: Due Soon

- **Content** — Cards or rows for loans due in the next 7 days: book title, due date, copy code, author.
- **Pagination** — Per-page selector and page navigation. Empty state: "No books due in the next week.".

---

## Tab 4: Loan History

- **Filters** — **From Date** and **To Date** (date pickers). Only loans with loan date in this range are shown. Reset clears dates.
- **Content** — List or table of loans: book, copy, borrower, loan date, due date, returned date (if any), status, etc. Pagination.

---

## Tab 5: Most Borrowed

- **Content** — List of books sorted by number of loans (highest first). Each row: book title, author, loan count. Pagination.

---

## Export Options

- **Books Report** — CSV export of the current filtered book list. Columns typically include: Title, Author, ISBN, Book Number, Category, Price, Total Copies, Available Copies, Default Loan Days. Filters (search, category) apply to the exported data.

---

## Tips & Best Practices

- Use **Overdue** and **Due Soon** regularly to follow up on returns.
- Use **Loan History** with a date range for term or monthly reports.
- Export **Books Report** to CSV for inventory or sharing with staff.
